Marsala

Description

Here is my paleo recipe on the traditional chicken Marsala! Great dish for entertaining and preparing in advance. Stress free.

Ingredients

  • Clove of garlic x 2
  • Potato x 2
  • Meat x 500g
  • Marsala wine x 250ml
  • Beef bouillon cube x 1
  • Champi√±ones mushrooms x 1 can
  • Butter x 50g
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley
  • Onion x 1
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Oil x tablespoon

Steps

  1. Chop the garlic clove and onion.
  2. Cut the meat into cubes
  3. Dissolve the beef bouillon cube in warm water.
  4. Put the chopped garlic in a saucepan with oil over low heat.
  5. When the garlic begins to brown, add the onion and stir.
  6. When the onion is soft, add the butter and the dissolved beef bouillon cube dissolved
  7. When the butter is melted, add the meat and the wine and turn up the heat using a lid to make it boil faster.
  8. Once boiling, lower the heat and leave uncovered for 15 to 20 minutes for the alcohol to evaporate./li>
  9. Once the alcohol has evaporated, add 1 tablespoon of flour, a little milk and the mushrooms.
  10. leave it on the fire for about 5 minutes and it is ready to serve.
  11. French fries make a great side dish.
