Marsala Steak

Description

Here is one of my favorite dishes, a pretty simple, pretty fast to make dish, that can fit well in most of the occasions.

Ingredients

  • Clove of garlic x 2
  • Potato x 2
  • Meat x 500g
  • Marsala wine x 250ml
  • Beef bouillon cube x 1
  • Champignon mushrooms x 1 can
  • Butter x 50g
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley
  • Onion x 1
  • Flour 1 spoon
  • Milk x 30ml
  • Oil x tablespoon

Steps

  1. Chop the garlic clove and onion.
  2. Cut the meat into cubes
  3. Dissolve the beef bouillon cube in warm water.
  4. Put the chopped garlic in a saucepan with oil over low heat.
  5. When the garlic begins to brown, add the onion and stir.
  6. When the onion is soft, add the butter and the dissolved beef bouillon cube dissolved
  7. Put salt, pepper and parsley to your liking, take notice that the beef bouillon adds salt to the mix too
  8. When the butter is melted, add the meat,the wine and turn up the heat using a lid to make it boil faster.
  9. Once boiling, lower the heat and leave uncovered for 15 to 20 minutes for the alcohol to evaporate.
  10. Once the alcohol has evaporated, add 1 tablespoon of flour, a little milk and the mushrooms.
  11. leave it on the fire for about 5 minutes and it is ready to serve.
  12. French fries make a great side dish.
