Lentil Stew

Description

The lentil stew is a terrific food, has pretty much every nutrient that could need and more, and is delicious.

Ingredients

  • Meat x 500g
  • Red Chorizo x 1
  • bacon x 100g
  • Lentils x 1kg
  • Tomato paste x 500 mg
  • Clove of garlic x 3
  • Onion x 1
  • Red bell pepper x ½
  • Green bell pepper x ¼
  • Tomato x 1
  • Potato x 2
  • Sweet potato x 1
  • Carrot x 1
  • Pumpkin x 300g
  • Parsley x 20g
  • Paprika x 2 teaspoon
  • Oil x 30ml
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • beef bouillon cube
  • beans x 1 can (precooked)

Steps

  1. Dice potatoes, carrot, pumpkin, tomato and sweet potato
  2. Cut the garlic cloves and put them in cup with the oil
  3. Finely chop the onion, red and green bell peppers
  4. Dice the meat, chorizo and bacon
  5. Put the garlic in a hot pot, low heat until it starts to brown
  6. Add the onion, green and red bell peppers, mix, put a lid and cook till the onion is soft
  7. Mix the meat, chorizo, tomato and bacon, season to taste, with salt, pepper, add the paprika and parsley
  8. Wait for 5 minutes
  9. Add the beef bouillon cube
  10. Add the lentils, tomato paste and water, turn the heat up, put the lid on and wait until it boils
  11. Once boiling, turn the heat down and stir the stew every 10 or 20 minutes
  12. 50-60 minutes in you can start checking for the lentils to be soft
  13. Add the beans, wait 5 minutes and it's done, bon appetit
